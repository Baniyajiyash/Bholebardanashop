A Name Farrukhabad Trusts

Bhole Bardana Shop, owned by Shiv Kumar Gupta, is a trusted name in Farrukhabad dealing in high-quality bardana products and gas accessories. For years we have served local shopkeepers, traders and households with reliable material, honest pricing and dependable service.

From jute and polyplast bardana to a complete range of gas fittings, we bring an industrial-grade catalogue with the care of a neighbourhood store — every product checked, every price fair, every customer valued.
