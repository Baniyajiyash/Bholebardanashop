Our Story

A shop built on what the mohalla actually needs

Bhole Bardana Shop sits in the Main Market of Yaqut Ganj, right by Shiv Mandir — a spot people have known for years. It's a store, not a showroom: shelves are stacked for use, not display, and stock moves the way it should in a working neighbourhood market.

Whether you're a shopkeeper restocking bardana for the season or a household needing a cylinder refilled, the counter is the same one it's always been.
